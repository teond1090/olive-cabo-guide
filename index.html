The July Rhythm

July in Cabo has a rhythm — locals swear by it. Follow this and the heat never wins.

🌅 Mornings · 7–11 am

Glass-calm water and the best visibility. Book every boat, snorkel and adventure tour in this window — the sea is at its absolute best before noon.

😎 Midday · 12–4 pm

Heat peaks near 91–96°F. Pool time, shaded-lunch time, KidZ Club time. Air conditioning is a Cabo activity. Drink more water than you think you need.

🌇 Evenings · 5 pm+

The town comes alive. Sunset cruises, marina strolls, dinner with toes in the sand. Quick July showers (if any) pass in under an hour.

🛟 Good-to-Knows

  • Swim where locals swim. Medano Beach & Chileno Bay are calm and swimmable; Pacific-side beaches have strong currents — watch the flag system.
  • Drink bottled or filtered water. Resorts filter their ice; bottled is the easy rule everywhere else.
  • Negotiate kindly. Water taxis and beach vendors expect a friendly haggle — start around 60–70% of the first price, with a smile.
  • July = baby sea-turtle season is coming. Give marked nests space. 🐢
  • Emergency number is 911, same as home.
